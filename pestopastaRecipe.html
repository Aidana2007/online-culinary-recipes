Pesto pasta

A crisp pastry shell filled with custard and topped with fresh cherries.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz dark chocolate
  • 2 eggs + 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Grease 4 ramekins.
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together in a bowl.
  3. Whisk eggs, yolks, and sugar until light.
  4. Fold in melted chocolate, then flour.
  5. Divide into ramekins and bake for 12 minutes.
  6. Serve warm with ice cream or berries.
