Latte

A decadent dessert with a warm, flowing chocolate center.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons canned pumpkin puree
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger brewed espresso
Copied to clipboard!

Portions

1 serving

Cooking Time

10 minutes

Instructions

  1. Whisk milk, pumpkin, sugar, vanilla, and pumpkin pie spice together in a small saucepan; warm over medium heat, whisking constantly, until hot and frothy.
  2. Pour brewed espresso into a mug and add pumpkin spiced milk over top. Serve.
