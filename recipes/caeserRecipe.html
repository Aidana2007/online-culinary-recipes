Caeser Salad

for when you want to impress your dinner guests.

Ingredients

  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Portions

2 servings

Cooking Time

20 minutes

Instructions

  1. Mash anchovy fillets and garlic in a large salad bowl.
  2. Add lemon juice, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, egg yolk, and Worcestershire sauce
  3. Gradually stream in olive oil while whisking constantly. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.
  4. Gently mix romaine lettuce and Parmesan cheese into dressing until thoroughly coated.
  5. Top Caesar salad with croutons and enjoy!
