Cheese omelette

This cheese omelette is great for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk, or as needed
  • salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ¼ cup shredded Emmentaler cheese
Copied to clipboard!

Portions

1 servings

Cooking Time

10 minutes

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs in a bowl; add milk and season with salt and white pepper.
  2. Whisk until egg mixture is foamy, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Melt butter in a small, nonstick skillet over medium-low heat.
  4. Pour in egg mixture and swirl the skillet, so the bottom is evenly covered with egg.
  5. Cook until egg starts to set, about 1 minute.
  6. Lift edges with a spatula and tilt the skillet so uncooked egg can flow towards the bottom of the skillet to set.
  7. Repeat until no visible liquid egg remains.
  8. Carefully flip omelette; cook until warmed through, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  9. Sprinkle Emmentaler cheese down the middle of omelette; fold in half.
