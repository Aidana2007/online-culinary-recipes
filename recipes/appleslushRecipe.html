Apple slush

Enjoy the fair at home with this apple-flavor slushy.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12-ounce) container frozen apple juice concentrate
  • 3 cups ice
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon apple pie spice, plus more for garnish (optional)
  • air fried apple slices, for garnish

Portions

4 servings

Cooking Time

5 minutes

Instructions

  1. Combine apple juice concentrate, ice, water, lemon juice, and apple pie spice in a blender; blend until mixture reaches desired consistency.
  2. Garnish with air-fried apple slices and additional apple pie spice.
  3. Preheat air fryer to 200 degrees F (93 degrees C).
  4. Coat 1/8-inch-thick apple slices with cooking spray; arrange in an even layer in air fryer basket.
  5. Cook, turning every 5 to 10 minutes
  6. Transfer cooked apples to a paper towel (apples will crisp as they cool).
